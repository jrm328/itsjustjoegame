Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

It’s Just Joe Games (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to respecting your privacy. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Information you provide: When you contact us through our form or by email, we receive your name, email, and any message you submit.
  • Basic usage data: Our website may collect non-identifying analytics such as pages visited, browser type, and approximate location.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide support
  • To improve our website and games
  • To maintain site security and performance

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers.
  • We do not collect financial information.

Your Rights

You may request that we update or delete your information at any time by emailing info@itsjustjoe.games.

Data Retention

We keep contact form submissions only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry, unless required otherwise by law.

Third-Party Services

Our site may use analytics or embedded content (e.g., YouTube videos) that follow their own privacy practices. We do not control those services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to our practices. The date above will always show the most recent version.
